#This is a VIM Tutorial 

##First Lesson - **Mastering the Up Down Left Right Keys**
- **h** for moving left
- **j** for moving down 
- **k** for moving top 
- **l** for moving right

##Second Lesson - **Mastering how to quit without saving**
- Use '**:q!**'

##Third Lesson - **To delete a character**
- Use '**x**'
